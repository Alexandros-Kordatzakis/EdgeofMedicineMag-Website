About Us

Edge of Medicine is a student-led science journal of the European University Cyprus, dedicated to presenting the latest research findings in the realm of medical sciences.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between cutting-edge medical research and the broader community, fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation of the advancements in medical science.

Our Vision

To be a leading source of reliable, impactful, and accessible medical research information, inspiring future generations of scientists and healthcare professionals.
